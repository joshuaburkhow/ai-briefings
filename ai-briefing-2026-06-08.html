Concepts to know

Agentic Development Lifecycle (ADLC)
Alpha Signal

A reframing of software development where AI agents generate most code and human developers act as orchestrators who validate architecture, define tests, and decide which code to keep or discard.

Recursive self-improvement
The Neuron Daily

When an AI system becomes capable of improving its own design or training without human oversight, potentially accelerating capability gains rapidly; cited by Anthropic as the risk behind its pause call.

Proof of personhood
The Neuron Daily

Cryptographic or biometric methods to verify that an online actor is a real, unique human rather than a bot, becoming urgent now that bots generate a majority of internet traffic.
