Tiny recursive models beat frontier LLMs on hard logic puzzles

  • A 27-million-parameter Hierarchical Reasoning Model (HRM) hit state-of-the-art on ARC-AGI puzzles using only 1,000 training examples, with up to a 100x reasoning speedup by working in latent space.Alpha Signal
  • Samsung researchers' Tiny Recursive Model (TRM), just 5–7M parameters, scored 87.4% on Sudoku-Extreme—versus 55.0% for HRM and 0.0% for DeepSeek-R1—plus 45% on ARC-AGI-1 and 85% on hard maze navigation.Alpha Signal
  • The key finding: increasing recursive steps, not adding layers, maximized generalization.Alpha Signal
  • A related RecursiveMAS multi-agent framework passing latent representations instead of text reported a 2.4x inference speedup and 75.6% fewer tokens by the third recursion round.Alpha Signal
Why it matters

Models thousands of times smaller than frontier LLMs beating them on deterministic logic points to a genuine architectural shift toward latent-space reasoning. If looping through internal memory beats generating tokens for logic tasks, it reframes both the scaling debate and the economics of reasoning.

Concepts to know

Latent-space reasoning
Alpha Signal

Reasoning by looping through a model's internal continuous memory states rather than generating text tokens; enables tiny recursive models like HRM and TRM to solve hard logic puzzles far faster than large LLMs.

Recursive / hierarchical reasoning model
Alpha Signal

An architecture that reuses a small weight-shared network over many recursive steps (e.g., HRM's high- and low-level modules); Samsung's TRM showed adding recursive steps, not layers, drives generalization.

Professional-grade AGI
Brain Buzz

Mustafa Suleyman's term for Microsoft's target of AI agents that can independently complete multi-step professional workflows in fields like law, accounting, and marketing.

Autonomous-cyber time horizon
The Neuron

A measure (used by the UK AI Safety Institute) of how long a chain of hacking steps a model can complete on its own; reported to have doubled on the order of months, with Mythos clearing a 32-step attack range.
