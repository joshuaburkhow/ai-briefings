Concepts to know

Dynamic workflows

A Claude Code capability where the model plans a task, spins up hundreds of parallel subagents in one session, verifies their output, and completes large multi-step jobs like codebase migrations end to end.

Post-money valuation
The Signal

A company's value immediately after a funding round, counting the new investment; Anthropic's $965B figure includes its fresh $65B Series H raise.

Agent governance
The Neuron Daily

The controls and oversight a company puts in place to manage the risks of autonomous AI agents acting on its behalf; a Deloitte survey found only 21% of firms have mature governance for it.

Tokenmaxxing
The Neuron Daily

The behavior of employees or teams maximizing AI token consumption, sometimes chasing usage metrics, which can drive runaway spending disconnected from actual output.
