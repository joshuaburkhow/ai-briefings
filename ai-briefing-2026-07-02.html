Concepts to know

Mixture of Experts (MoE)
TLDR AI

A neural network architecture that routes each input token through only a subset of specialized expert sub-networks rather than activating the full model. Makes trillion-parameter models like LongCat-2.0 practical — only a fraction of parameters fire per token, keeping compute cost closer to a much smaller dense model.

Flash-tier models
TLDR AI

A model tier optimized for speed and cost efficiency at some capability ceiling trade-off. Nano Banana 2 Lite and Gemini Omni Flash are Google's flash-tier entries for image and video generation — fast enough for real-time creative pipelines and priced for high-volume enterprise use.

Agentic performance
The Deep View

A model's ability to plan multi-step tasks, select and use tools such as browsers, terminals, and APIs, self-correct errors, and operate autonomously without per-step human direction. Distinct from benchmark scores — it measures sustained reliability across long, tool-heavy workflows rather than single-turn accuracy.
