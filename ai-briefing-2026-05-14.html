INDUSTRY

Thinking Machines details TML-Interaction-Small, a real-time multimodal model

  • Thinking Machines released TML-Interaction-Small, a 276-billion-parameter real-time model that listens, speaks and watches video simultaneously, processing input and output in 200-millisecond chunks.Alpha Signal
  • It uses a two-part architecture, a live 'interaction layer' for timing and cues plus a 'background layer' for web searches, reasoning and tool calls, tracking when a user is pausing or inviting a response with no hardcoded dialogue rules.Alpha Signal
Why it matters

The technical design shows how a model can hold a natural, interruptible real-time conversation. Splitting timing and turn-taking from heavier reasoning lets the system respond instantly while still searching and reasoning in the background, a template for responsive multimodal assistants.

MIT study warns of 'cognitive debt' from over-reliance on LLMs

  • An MIT EEG study of 54 participants over four months found the group using ChatGPT to write showed up to 55% reduced neural connectivity.Alpha Signal
  • 83% of the LLM users could not quote from essays they had just written, and researchers coined the term 'cognitive debt.'Alpha Signal
  • When brain-only writers later switched to AI, their connectivity increased, suggesting AI used after sufficient self-driven effort could enhance rather than harm learning.Alpha Signal
Why it matters

A controlled study puts hard numbers on the cognitive cost of offloading thinking to AI. Measurable drops in neural connectivity and memory of one's own work suggest how AI is used matters as much as whether it is used, with implications for education and knowledge work.

Google's cross-device AI platform for Android that carries out agentic tasks within and across apps, browses the web, fills forms, uses on-screen context and generates custom widgets from natural language, replacing the single bolted-on chatbot.
