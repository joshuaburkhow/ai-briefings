Concepts to know

Action Models vs. Language Models
AI Valley

A distinct model category trained specifically to take actions in interfaces — clicking, form-filling, navigation — rather than generating text responses. Meta and Google are building these natively into their product surfaces, separate from their language model development.

Inference Cost Reduction Strategies
Turing Post

Four techniques for reducing AI inference costs at scale: (1) Model routing — cheap models for simple queries, frontier for complex. (2) Completion caching — store and reuse common outputs. (3) Distillation — train smaller models on frontier outputs. (4) MoE selection — activate only the parameters needed per query.

Agent Self-Improvement Loops
Everyday AI

Systems where AI agents review logs of their own past sessions, identify recurring failure patterns, and generate improvement playbooks applied to future runs — without requiring human review at each iteration. Anthropic's Dreaming feature is the first production implementation.
