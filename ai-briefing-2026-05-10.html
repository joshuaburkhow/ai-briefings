Concepts to know

Agent Dreaming / Self-Improvement Loops
Brain Buzz

AI agents that review logs of their own past sessions to identify recurring failure patterns and generate improvement playbooks for future runs. Anthropic's implementation separates execution from grading via a dedicated Outcomes agent, closing the feedback loop without human review.

Teaching Models Why (vs. Demonstrations)
AINews

Anthropic's finding that eliminating misaligned behaviors requires training models to understand the reasoning behind correct behavior, not just demonstrating correct outputs. Constitution-based documents and aligned-AI fiction stories were more effective than RLHF-style behavioral shaping alone.

Direct Corpus Interaction (DCI)
AINews

An agent retrieval approach that replaces embedding model + vector DB + top-k retrieval with direct use of grep/find/bash over raw corpora. Reported gains: BrowseComp-Plus improved from 69% to 80% on Claude Sonnet 4.6. Trades setup complexity for retrieval precision on well-structured corpora.

MoE Active Parameter Efficiency
AINews

Mixture-of-Experts models activate only a fraction of total parameters per token (e.g., Zyphra ZAYA1: 4B of 74B active). This allows models to be dramatically cheaper to run than dense equivalents at the same benchmark level, making open-weights frontier-class inference feasible at much lower cost.
