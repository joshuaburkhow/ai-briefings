Concepts to know

All-stock deal
The Neuron Daily

An acquisition paid entirely in the buyer's shares rather than cash; SpaceX is buying Cursor with $60 billion of SpaceX stock, tying Cursor's value to SpaceX's.

Bidirectional (full-duplex) audio
AI Breakfast

A voice architecture that lets a model listen and speak at the same time and handle interruptions naturally, as in OpenAI's planned GPT-Bidi-1, unlike turn-based voice systems.

Android MCP / AppFunctions
TLDR AI

New Android 17 interfaces that let apps expose callable tools which on-device AI agents can discover and orchestrate, turning the phone into an agent-executable platform.

World model
The Neuron Daily

A model that learns to predict how an environment changes in response to actions; Qwen-RobotWorld uses one so robots can plan navigation and manipulation from natural-language commands.
