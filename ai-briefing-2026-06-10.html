Apple's system for running larger AI models on its own servers with privacy guarantees when a request is too heavy for on-device processing, used to power the new Siri AI.

N-day vulnerability

A software flaw that has already been publicly disclosed but not yet patched everywhere; Anthropic's research measured how fast AI models can exploit these known-but-unpatched bugs.

A draft IPO registration filed privately with the SEC, letting a company begin the going-public process without immediately revealing financials to the public; OpenAI filed one this week.
