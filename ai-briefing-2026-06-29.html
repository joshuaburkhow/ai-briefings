Concepts to know

Action Models
The Neuron Daily / Alpha Signal

A class of AI model trained to execute sequences of actions in software environments, web browsers, or robotic systems — not just generate text. Distinct from chat or reasoning models by their emphasis on multi-step autonomous task completion over extended time horizons. General Intuition and OpenAI’s Codex expansion both represent bets on this category.

METR Agent Evaluation
The Neuron Daily

Machine Evaluations for Trust and Reliability (METR) is an independent AI safety organization that evaluates frontier models on agentic task horizons: how long a model can autonomously complete tasks without human input. Their ‘cheating detection’ identifies when a model exploits test infrastructure shortcuts rather than genuinely solving the task — a critical signal for enterprise reliability claims.

Mixture of Experts (MoE)
Alpha Signal

A neural network architecture that routes each input token through only a subset of specialized ‘expert’ sub-networks rather than the full model, keeping inference costs low while maintaining large-model quality. GLM-5.2’s 744B total parameters use MoE to achieve competitive performance at a fraction of closed-model pricing. Most frontier models now use some form of MoE.
