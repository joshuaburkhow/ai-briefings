Concepts to know

Business adoption share

The metric tracked by Ramp's AI Index, measuring the percentage of businesses using a given AI provider; it is the basis for the finding that Anthropic passed OpenAI, whose share fell to 32.3% as overall AI use reached 50.6%.

Multi-agent security system
TLDR AI

An architecture where many specialized AI agents collaborate, one group scanning code, another debating whether findings are exploitable, and a final stage building proof-of-concept attacks, as in Microsoft's 100-plus-agent MDASH.

CyberGym benchmark
The Rundown

A benchmark that measures how well an AI system can reproduce real-world software vulnerabilities, used to compare Microsoft's MDASH against Anthropic's Mythos model.

Self-improving AI
Everyday AI

Models that experiment on and upgrade themselves with little human input; the thesis behind Recursive Superintelligence's raise of more than $650 million at a valuation above $4 billion.
