Concepts to know

Reverse Information Paradox
GenAI.works / AI Secret

Satya Nadella's term for a structural data-liability risk in enterprise AI adoption: organizations pay for intelligence in cash, then pay again by feeding proprietary prompts, evaluations, and corrections to model vendors who can use that behavioral exhaust to learn how the business operates. The more effectively a company uses AI, the more competitive knowledge it inadvertently transfers to its AI provider.

Task Graph Decomposition
Alpha Signal

A method for breaking complex multi-step AI agent tasks into structured dependency graphs whose sub-tasks each fit within a smaller model's effective working-memory window. Instead of asking one model to maintain a long reasoning chain in context — where compact models typically fail — the approach parcels work into bounded sub-problems, enabling 8B-parameter models to match GPT-4-scale performance on agent benchmarks without any additional training.

HBM (High Bandwidth Memory)
AI Secret

The specialized memory architecture inside AI accelerators like Nvidia's H100 and H200 GPUs. HBM stacks multiple memory die directly atop the processor using through-silicon vias, delivering memory bandwidth far higher than conventional DRAM. HBM supply — dominated by SK Hynix and Samsung — is one of the primary bottlenecks in AI infrastructure scaling, making HBM chip stocks a closely watched proxy for AI industry health.
