Concepts to know

Inference chip
The Neuron Daily / The Deep View

A chip optimized for running AI models in response to queries (inference), as opposed to training chips that learn from data. Jalapeño is an inference chip: it makes ChatGPT faster and cheaper to serve, but doesn’t train new models.

Computer use (agents)
The Deep View

The ability for an AI agent to directly control a desktop or browser interface — clicking, typing, navigating — the way a human would. Gemini 3.5 Flash gaining this capability brings it into the mainstream alongside Claude.

Full-stack vertical integration
The Deep View

A strategy where a company controls every layer of its product stack — for AI, this means owning the chip (infrastructure), the model, and the consumer product. OpenAI’s Jalapeño move is a step toward this structure, similar to Apple’s control of silicon, OS, and device.
