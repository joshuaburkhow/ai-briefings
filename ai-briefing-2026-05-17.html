Concepts to know

Multi-Token Prediction (MTP)
AINews

A decoding technique where a model predicts several tokens at once and accepts them if they pass a check; a llama.cpp fork reported ~+62% throughput on Qwen 3.6 with a 90% acceptance rate.

FP8 KV-cache quantization
AINews

Storing the attention key/value cache in 8-bit floating point; a vLLM study found it roughly doubles KV-cache capacity with negligible accuracy loss, making it a strong production default.

Neocloud
The Deep View

A specialized GPU cloud provider (like Lambda) built specifically for AI training and inference, distinct from the general-purpose hyperscalers.
