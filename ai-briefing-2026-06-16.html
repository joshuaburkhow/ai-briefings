Concepts to know

Export-control directive
The Rundown AI

A government order restricting who may access a technology; here it required Anthropic to block all non-US citizens, even those inside the country and its own foreign-national staff, forcing a global shutdown.

Model fusion / mixing
The Rundown AI

Combining outputs from several models into one answer; OpenRouter's Fusion pooled three cheaper models to nearly match a single frontier model's benchmark score at half the cost.

BIRD benchmark
AI Breakfast

A standard test of natural-language-to-SQL systems measured by execution accuracy; Gemini-SQL2's 80.04% score reflects how reliably it turns plain-English questions into correct database queries.

Jailbreak
AI Breakfast

A technique that bypasses a model's safety guardrails; the Fable 5 jailbreak reportedly combined Unicode variations, long-context tracking, and multi-agent reconstruction to extract restricted information.
