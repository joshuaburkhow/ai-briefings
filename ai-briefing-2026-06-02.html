Concepts to know

Confidential S-1 filing
Everyday AI

A draft registration statement a company submits privately to the SEC to begin the IPO process without publicly disclosing its finances until closer to the offering.

World model
The Deep View

An AI model that learns how the physical world behaves across modalities like video, sound and actions, so robots and autonomous vehicles can simulate and plan; Nvidia's Cosmos 3 is one example.

Open-weights model
TLDR AI

A model whose trained parameters are released publicly so anyone can run or fine-tune it locally, as with MiniMax M3, distinct from closed models accessible only via API.

Always-on agent
TLDR AI

An AI agent that runs continuously in the background rather than only when prompted; Microsoft's leaked 'Scout' is described as one that may operate remotely.
