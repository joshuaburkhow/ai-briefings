Concepts to know

Frontier-model audit
The Neuron / AI Breakfast

A structured pre-release evaluation of an advanced model for dangerous cyber, biological, deceptive, or autonomous capabilities. Hassabis proposes a 30-day submission window and an independent industry-funded testing body.

Ternary weights
TLDR AI

A model-compression approach where weights use three values, usually negative, zero, and positive. It can shrink memory requirements enough for much larger models to run on phones, with accuracy and hardware-support tradeoffs.

Compute forward curve
TLDR AI

A market view of expected future GPU-rental prices. Kalshi plans to derive one from weekly and monthly prediction contracts, treating compute capacity more like a traded commodity.
