ANTHRO

Claude Managed Agents Hit 96% of Frontier Performance at 46% of the Cost

  • Anthropic published two multi-agent cost patterns. Advisor pattern: Sonnet 5 handles all work and calls Fable 5 only at key decision points — scores 92% of Fable 5 on SWE-bench Pro at 63% of the cost.AlphaSignal
  • Orchestrator pattern: Fable 5 plans the task, Sonnet 5 workers execute in parallel — reaches 96% of Fable 5's performance at 46% of the price. Real example: verifying 20 facts cost $1.61 vs. $4.00 solo.AlphaSignal / The Neuron
  • Each sub-agent maintains its own prompt cache, so repeated context is not billed twice. Working code for both patterns is in the Claude Cookbooks repo.AlphaSignal
Why it matters

Running Fable 5 on every token is now wasteful by design. For anyone building production Claude agents, switching to the Orchestrator or Advisor pattern should be the immediate next step. The 46%-cost result on BrowseComp is the headline, but the per-agent caching bonus compounds on long workflows.

Concepts to know

Full-duplex voice
TLDR AI / The Deep View

A voice architecture where a model can listen and speak at the same time — like a phone call rather than a walkie-talkie. Eliminates the awkward turn-taking pause of previous voice assistants and enables real-time interruption, live translation, and continuous conversation flow.

Claude Managed Agents
AlphaSignal

Anthropic's framework for running multi-agent workflows where Fable 5 orchestrates and cheaper submodels (Sonnet 5) execute tasks. Each sub-agent maintains its own prompt cache, cutting repeated-context billing. Enables near-frontier quality at significantly lower cost per task.

SWE-Bench Pro noise ceiling
AlphaSignal / TLDR AI

The point at which a benchmark's broken or ambiguous tasks set a hard upper bound on scores regardless of model quality. OpenAI's audit found SWE-Bench Pro hits this ceiling around 70%, meaning frontier models may score near 70% even if every solvable task is solved correctly — making rankings above that level unreliable.

Orchestrator pattern
AlphaSignal

A multi-agent design where a capable (expensive) model plans and delegates, while cheaper worker models run tasks in parallel. Anthropic's implementation reaches 96% of Fable 5's solo performance at 46% of the cost by having Fable 5 orchestrate and Sonnet 5 workers execute.
