Concepts to know

Model distillation
AI Breakfast

Training a smaller model to imitate a larger one's outputs, effectively copying its capabilities; Anthropic secretly routed distillation-related prompts away from Fable 5 to prevent competitors from cloning it.

Diffusion language model
AI Breakfast

A text model that generates by iteratively refining many tokens in parallel from random noise, rather than predicting one token at a time left to right; Google's DiffusionGemma is an open example.

Synthetic employee
AI Breakfast

A framing in which an autonomous AI agent acting inside a company, such as trading or moving money, is governed like a human employee with accountability and oversight, as a global banking watchdog has proposed.
