Concepts to know

Performance-per-watt
Everyday AI

A measure of how much AI computation a chip does per unit of energy; OpenAI's Jalapeño reportedly leads on it, which matters because power is the main limit on scaling inference.

Model routing / orchestration
Alpha Signal

Automatically selecting and combining different AI models for each step of a task; Sakana's Fugu does this through one endpoint and can swap models to sidestep export controls.

OCR (optical character recognition)
TLDR AI

Technology that extracts text and structure from documents and images; Mistral OCR 4 does it across 170 languages with bounding boxes and confidence scores at claimed 4x speed.

Post-quantum encryption
The Neuron Daily

Cryptography designed to resist future quantum computers; Trump's executive orders require federal agencies to migrate to it by 2030-2031 to protect against 'harvest now, decrypt later' threats.
