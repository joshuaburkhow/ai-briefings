Concepts to know

Speech-to-speech translation
The Neuron Daily

AI that converts spoken words in one language directly into spoken words in another as a continuous audio stream, rather than transcribing, translating text, then re-speaking in separate steps.

Token price war
TLDR AI

Competing AI providers repeatedly undercutting each other's per-token usage prices to win customers, which can compress already-thin margins for companies losing billions on compute.

Brain-computer interface (BCI)
TLDR AI

A device that reads neural signals and lets a person control external hardware, like a robotic glove, with their thoughts; China's NEO is a coin-sized version cleared for commercial use.
