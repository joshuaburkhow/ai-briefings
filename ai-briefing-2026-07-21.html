Concepts to know

Open-weight model
The Neuron / AI Breakfast

A model whose trained parameters can be downloaded and run outside the vendor's hosted service. Open weights improve control but do not guarantee low serving cost or full training transparency.

Inference stack
TLDR AI

The software and infrastructure that package a trained model and serve responses through production APIs. It includes the engine plus deployment design plus constraints plus monitoring.

Compute lease
AI Breakfast / Everyday AI

A contract for access to another company's data-center capacity. Frontier labs use these arrangements to add accelerators faster than they can build their own facilities.
