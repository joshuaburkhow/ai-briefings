Concepts to know

100x Token Problem
BrainBuzz / AI Breakfast

The phenomenon where agentic AI workflows — agents looping, calling tools, re-reading context, retrying — consume tokens at roughly 100 times the rate of a single-turn query, making per-token pricing an unreliable proxy for actual AI infrastructure cost in production.

Cyber Safeguard Bypass
Human in the Loop

A jailbreak targeting an AI model's safety controls around cybersecurity tasks specifically — distinct from general content policy violations. The Amazon-discovered bypass in Fable 5 was severe enough to trigger US export controls on the model pending Anthropic's remediation.

Terminal-Bench
Human in the Loop

A benchmark suite testing AI agents on long-horizon terminal tasks with dense reward-based grading. Unlike single-shot coding tests, it measures sustained autonomous operation across multi-step command-line workflows — the battleground both OpenAI and Anthropic now cite for competing benchmark claims.
