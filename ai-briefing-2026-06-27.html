Concepts to know

Red-teaming
TLDR AI

Structured adversarial testing where experts try to make a model misbehave or reveal dangerous capabilities; the White House wants an extended red-teaming window before OpenAI's next model ships.

Computer use
The Neuron Daily

A model capability to see a screen and control software by clicking and typing; Google built it into Gemini 3.5 Flash so agents can operate browsers, apps, and desktops directly.

World model
Alpha Signal

A model that learns to simulate how an environment behaves rather than just act in it; Qwen-AgentWorld uses one to train agents in simulation across seven digital domains.

DRAM shortage
The Neuron Daily

A scarcity of dynamic memory chips, driven partly by AI demand; prices more than doubling since October 2025 forced Apple to raise Mac and iPad prices.
