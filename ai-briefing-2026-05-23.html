Concepts to know

Environment scaling

A training approach used for Qwen3.7-Max that emphasizes learning to act across many tool environments, enabling long-horizon autonomous execution (up to 35 hours) without falling into logic loops.

Managed Agents

Goal mode

An OpenAI Codex mode (now generally available) where a user sets an objective and Codex works toward it autonomously for hours or days, with periodic check-ins.
