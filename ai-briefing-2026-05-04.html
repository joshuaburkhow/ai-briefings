Concepts to know

Benefit Corporation Structure
Brain Buzz

A legal corporate structure that allows a for-profit company to pursue social or mission-driven goals alongside shareholder returns. OpenAI's restructure creates a benefit corporation for commercial operations, preserving the nonprofit's governance authority while enabling equity compensation and external investment.

Orchestration Engine vs. Scaffolding Framework
Brain Buzz

An orchestration engine (Mistral, Agentspace) is a managed platform for deploying and connecting multiple agents in production, with built-in routing, memory, and governance. A scaffolding framework (LangChain, LlamaIndex) is a developer library for building agent logic — the lower-level abstraction that orchestration engines are replacing.

Agent Trust Escalation
Brain Buzz

The progressive expansion of autonomy granted to AI agents — from supervised discrete tasks to unsupervised multi-step tasks to autonomous production system modifications. AlphaEvolve modifying Google ML infrastructure without per-change human review represents the highest public trust escalation disclosed by any major lab.
