Concepts to know

VL Reward Model
The Batch

A vision-language model trained to score how well a robot completed a task (on a 1-5 progress scale), used as a reward signal during reinforcement learning. Replaces expensive human evaluation with an automated judge that watches the robot act.

World Model
The Deep View

An AI system trained to understand and simulate the physical environment — geometry, physics, and interactive causality — rather than just text sequences. Proponents argue world models are necessary for AGI because language alone cannot represent physical consequence.

Transformer-Only ASIC
Neat Prompts

A chip designed exclusively to accelerate transformer-architecture inference, trading general-purpose flexibility for dramatic speed and efficiency gains. Etched’s Sohu claims 20x the throughput of H100 GPUs for Llama-style models, but becomes obsolete if the dominant architecture changes.

Steganographic Watermarking
AI Breakfast

Hidden patterns embedded in model outputs (such as invisible Unicode markers) to identify the source of training data. Anthropic removed such code from Claude Code after developers found it was being used to detect competitors attempting to distill Claude’s behavior into rival models.
