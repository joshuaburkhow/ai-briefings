Concepts to know

Sparse mixture of experts
TLDR AI / The Neuron

A model design that activates only selected specialist components for each request. Kimi K3 uses sparse experts to reduce runtime work despite its very large total parameter count.

Task-scoped credentials
The Deep View

Authentication authority granted only for one approved task and session. The agent can complete a login without reading or retaining the underlying secret.
