Concepts to know

HBM (High-Bandwidth Memory)
INDUSTRY

A type of memory chip stacked directly atop a GPU die, enabling far faster data transfer than standard DRAM. Critical for AI training because transformer models require moving enormous parameter sets between memory and compute every forward pass. HBM3E, used in Nvidia's H200/B200, is the current gold standard.

AI Consultant Certification
OPENAI

Structured credentialing programs that train practitioners to deploy and manage AI systems within enterprise environments. Analogous to Salesforce's Trailhead or AWS certifications — they create a professional ecosystem whose career value is tied to a specific platform, driving long-term enterprise lock-in.

Agentic Control Problem
INDUSTRY

The governance challenge of maintaining meaningful human oversight when AI agents act autonomously on behalf of organizations. Distinct from alignment (making AI want the right things) — this is about organizational controls, audit trails, and intervention mechanisms in production AI deployments.

Ambient Clinical Documentation
INDUSTRY

AI systems that passively listen to patient-physician conversations and automatically generate structured medical notes in the EHR. Addresses physician burnout from documentation burden — often cited as 30-40% of a physician's workday — without requiring manual dictation or structured data entry.
