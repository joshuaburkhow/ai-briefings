Concepts to know

Agentic scaffolding
AlphaSignal

The testing harness and infrastructure wrapped around an AI agent during evaluation or deployment — determines which actions the agent can take, how results are scored, and how errors are caught. Poor scaffolding systematically suppresses measured agent performance.

Drug discovery AI
BrainBuzz

AI systems purpose-built for pharmaceutical R&D: predicting protein structures, identifying drug candidates, modeling molecular interactions, and accelerating clinical trial design. AlphaFold (DeepMind) is the canonical example; Anthropic's drug discovery move extends this trend to general-purpose AI labs.

ROI reckoning
Mindstream

The current enterprise phase in which AI vendors must show measurable return on investment — revenue uplift, hours saved, error rates reduced — rather than relying on capability benchmarks or pilot enthusiasm to justify renewals and expansions.
