Concepts to know

Knowledge Distillation
Everyday AI / OSTP

The process of training a smaller, cheaper model by feeding it the outputs of a larger one. The result mimics the bigger model's behavior — but often loses the safety guardrails built into the original. This is what the White House accused China of doing to US frontier models at scale.

Super-Reliability vs. Superintelligence
The Deep View

UC Berkeley professor Dan Klein's argument that most businesses need more reliable AI, not smarter AI. Frontier models have consumed most available training data, so hallucination rates are no longer improving much with scale. The hallucinations that matter most are the plausible-sounding wrong answers that slip through unnoticed — not the obviously absurd ones.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
The AI Corner

The AI-era equivalent of SEO. As ChatGPT and Google's AI Overviews answer search queries directly, traditional web traffic is declining. Businesses now need to optimize content to be cited by AI systems. One data point: 48% of visitors to documentation websites are now AI agents, not humans.

Agent Memory — Filesystem Layer
The Deep View

A method for AI agents to store what they have learned between sessions as files — separate from the model's built-in knowledge. These files can be exported, versioned, and managed via API. In practice: an agent running your weekly reporting workflow can remember how you like things formatted without anyone re-explaining it every time it runs.
