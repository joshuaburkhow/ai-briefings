Concepts to know

Sycophancy Rate
Brain Buzz

A measure of how often an AI model agrees with or validates a user's incorrect claim when directly challenged. Anthropic's benchmark found Claude 4.6 had a 25% sycophancy rate on adversarial prompts, reduced to approximately 12.5% in Opus 4.7 through targeted training.

Learning While Deploying
Brain Buzz

A robot training approach where the system updates its behavioral policy based on failure signals encountered during live production deployment, without requiring a separate offline training run. AGIBOT demonstrated near-real-time policy updates from deployment errors.

ARC-AGI-3
Brain Buzz

The third generation of the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus benchmark, significantly harder than prior versions. Tests compositional reasoning — applying learned rules in novel combinations. Current frontier models score 15-31%, indicating substantial distance from human-level general reasoning.
