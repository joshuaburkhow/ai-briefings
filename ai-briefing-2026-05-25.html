Concepts to know

Self-distilled fine-tuning (SDFT)
Alpha Signal

A training technique used in Composer 2.5 that inserts a localized textual hint at the exact step where a model deviates from the correct path, teaching it to self-correct without relying on expensive frontier models.

Hybrid model architecture
Alpha Signal

An engineering pattern that routes 80–90% of routine work to cheap specialized models and reserves expensive frontier models for the 10–20% of deeply complex tasks, to control token spend.
