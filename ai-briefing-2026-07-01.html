Concepts to know

Speculative Decoding
TLDR AI

A technique where a fast ‘draft’ model generates token candidates ahead of the main model, which then validates them in parallel. Cuts per-token latency without changing output quality. DeepSeek DSpark uses it to achieve 85% inference speed gains.

MEG (Magnetoencephalography)
AlphaSignal

A non-invasive brain imaging method that records magnetic fields produced by electrical activity in neurons. Meta’s Brain2Qwerty v2 uses MEG instead of surgical implants to decode intended typed text at 78% accuracy.

CASI (Center for AI Standards and Innovation)
The AI Report

A US government testing unit under NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) that evaluates AI safety and security properties. CASI brokered the agreement that restored Anthropic’s Fable 5 global access after its safeguard breach.
