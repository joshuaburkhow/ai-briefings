Concepts to know

Reasoning model
Everyday AI

A model trained to work through problems step by step before answering, improving performance on complex tasks like software engineering; Microsoft's MAI-Thinking-1 is one example.

Mixture-of-transformers
The Deep View

An architecture that pairs separate transformers for different jobs, such as one for reasoning and one for generation, used in Nvidia's Cosmos 3 world model.

At-the-market offering
TLDR AI

A way for a public company to sell new shares gradually into the open market at prevailing prices; Alphabet plans a $40B ATM program as part of raising $80B for AI compute.

Search as Code
TLDR AI

Perplexity's approach of exposing search as a programmable interface, via an SDK, so an AI model can directly control how it queries and retrieves information.
