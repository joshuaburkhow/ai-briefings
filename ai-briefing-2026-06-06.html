Concepts to know

Recursive self-improvement
The Rundown AI

When an AI system can design and build a more capable successor with little or no human involvement; Anthropic warned Claude is nearing this as it authors most of the lab's own code.

Dreaming (memory synthesis)
The Rundown AI

A background process where an AI reviews and summarizes past conversations into an organized, refreshed user profile, rather than relying on the user to explicitly flag what to remember.

Red-teaming
TLDR AI

Giving trusted testers early access to a model to probe for flaws and misuse before public launch; Anthropic paused its program after a tester resold the leaked Oceanus checkpoint.

Physical AGI
The Deep View

The goal of building AI general enough to operate in the physical world through robots; the target of Generalist's $400M raise backed by Nvidia.
