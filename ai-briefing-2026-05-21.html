Concepts to know

Guaranteed Capacity
TLDR AI

OpenAI's new product letting customers lock in long-term compute via one-, two-, or three-year commitments, with discounts scaling by commitment length, offered until its allocation sells out.

Any-to-any world model
Alpha Signal

A model like Gemini Omni that understands physics and continuity to take any input (text, image, voice) and produce any output (notably video), editing scenes conversationally with consistent characters.

Parallel subagents
AI Breakfast

An architecture (as in Google Antigravity 2.0) where one goal is split across many coding agents running in isolated environments simultaneously; a demo used 93 subagents and 2.6 billion tokens to build an OS framework in 12 hours.

Spec-Driven Development
Alpha Signal

GitHub Spec Kit's approach of guiding AI coding agents through explicit spec, plan, and task steps (vs. ad-hoc 'vibe coding'), compatible with 30+ agents under an MIT license.
