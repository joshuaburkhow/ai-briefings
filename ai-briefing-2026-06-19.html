Concepts to know

World model

An AI system that learns an internal simulation of physical environments, letting it predict and generate how the world behaves over time rather than just processing text or images.

AI export controls

Government restrictions that limit who can access an advanced AI model, including blocking use outside a country or by foreign nationals, on national-security grounds.

Open agent framework
TLDR AI

An open-source toolkit for building, running, and scaling AI agents with built-in production features like durable execution, sandboxed compute, approvals, and evaluations.

Runtime credential exchange
TLDR AI

A security approach that replaces long-lived API tokens with short-lived, task-scoped credentials issued to agents at runtime to reduce the risk of leaked persistent access.
