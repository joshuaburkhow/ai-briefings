  • Warp open-sourced its AI terminal; the full Rust codebase crossed 37k stars on GitHub, with OpenAI as founding sponsor and GPT models powering the agent workflows.Alpha Signal
  • Cursor released an open-source TypeScript SDK to build and deploy coding agents outside the editor, running locally or on Cursor's cloud with its own VM per agent; early users include Rippling, Notion, Faire and C3 AI.Alpha Signal

INDUSTRY

Abstract Chain-of-Thought cuts reasoning tokens roughly 11x

  • A new training method, Abstract Chain-of-Thought, cuts LLM reasoning tokens by about 11.6x by reasoning through short sequences of special reserved tokens instead of English.Alpha Signal
  • It matches or beats standard verbal reasoning on math, multi-hop QA and instruction-following, and works across model families using two-stage training.Alpha Signal
Why it matters

Cutting reasoning tokens by an order of magnitude directly attacks the cost of inference-heavy models. As reasoning models drive up inference bills, compressing the chain of thought into abstract tokens could make advanced reasoning far cheaper to run at scale.

Concepts to know

Remaining performance obligations (RPO)
The Neuron

Signed commercial contracts that have not yet been billed or delivered; hyperscalers cite RPO backlogs (Microsoft $627B, Google roughly $462B) as a forward signal of committed AI and cloud demand.

Agent-native tooling
Alpha Signal

Developer tools—terminals, IDEs, CI—redesigned so autonomous multi-step agents, rather than humans, are the primary users, with tool use and long-running tasks treated as the default.

Abstract Chain-of-Thought
Alpha Signal

A reasoning technique where a model thinks through a short sequence of special reserved 'abstract' tokens instead of plain-English steps, cutting reasoning token cost by roughly 11x while preserving accuracy.

Dark agent sprawl
Everyday AI

The unmonitored proliferation of AI agents inside an organization—for example an employee spinning up dozens of coding-agent instances unseen by IT—an escalation of the 'shadow AI' problem.
