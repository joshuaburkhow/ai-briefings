Concepts to know

J-Space (Global Workspace)
The Rundown AI / Anthropic Research

A latent internal representation layer in language models, analogous to the Global Workspace theory of human consciousness. In Claude, J-space acts as a neural scratchpad for multi-step reasoning -- it exists in the weights, not in chain-of-thought text, and was never explicitly trained to exist.

Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)
TLDR AI

A neural network architecture where only a fraction of the total parameters activate per input token. Tencent's Hy3 has 295B total parameters but activates only ~21B per request -- achieving the quality of a large model at the inference cost of a small one.

AI Safety Audit
The Rundown AI

A structured third-party evaluation of an AI system's risks, misuse potential, and safety controls. Illinois HB 3506 requires annual audits for large AI developers -- the first US law to mandate this, setting the template other states may adopt.
